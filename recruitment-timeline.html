Who is the NYC Mayor’s Office of Economic Opportunity?

We help the city apply evidence and innovation to reduce poverty and increase equity.


We are optimistic that this work can be moved forward in government and have large scale impact. Our iterative nature allows us to re-imagine and re-design how government works. Our mission and values continue to evolve as we learn together.

What Happens From Offer to Start Date?

It may take approximately 2-4 months of internal processing from offer to start date. Due to the nature of government hiring, this onboarding time frame varies case by case. Becoming any kind of government employee requires multiple chains of review and approval.

As you navigate this process, we will check in to offer updates. But some common things that happen internally from offer to start date include:


  • Background checks
  • Budget approvals
  • Position in the queue
  • Review of experience against the City’s Civil Service Titles

Must have to apply


  • Resume
  • Cover Letter
  • Recruitment Form
  • City Job Site

  • Publications

What Do I Need To Apply?


Begin your process by exploring the open position descriptions, and getting to know the team you might best fit with. We pursue our work through five interrelated disciplines: *Research, Service Design, Digital Products, Data Integration, and Programs & Evaluation.* Each team will have a different set of required materials to apply.


In our hiring process, we strive to be less prescriptive and more open to what you want to share. The best way for us to know you better, and see the tangible aspects of your experience is if you can share more about your personal role and work.
