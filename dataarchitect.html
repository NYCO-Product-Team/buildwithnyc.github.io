MDM Data Architect

The Master Data Management Data Architect will manage will manage the Mayor’s Office of Operations centralized IBM MDM system, and an accompanying analytics architecture. S/he will manage development work including, but not limited to, the installation, configuration, and ongoing maintenance of the MDM system components. S/he will also work closely with City agencies, contracted service providers and vendors to perform data profiling, source system analysis and data modeling to make recommendations on how MDM can support data integration and analytics strategies.


  Responsibilities

  • Manage a large, citywide MDM platform support Party entity resolution across multiple data sources.
  • Support the development and refinement of underlying record linkage processes.
  • Coordinate the efforts on the technical architecture (infrastructure) design with consideration of capacity, performance, and high availability.
  • Create and update technical design specifications.
  • Support the design and development of dimensional models and data lakes within the City’s integrated data platform.
  • Work with internal and external stakeholders to understand their data and reporting needs, present them with various options and guide them to analytic solutions that best meets their needs.
  • Perform data profiling of legacy and strategic data source systems to assess data quality and identify issues, inconsistencies and missing data.
  • Develop protocols and governance that promote data standards and data quality across multiple city agencies.
  • Create and maintain excellent documentation including data dictionaries, source-to-target maps, and database policies and guidelines.
  • Collaborate with the application developers, ETL team and database administrators to ensure database performance, integrity and security.

  Skills We're Seeking

Commitment to the mission of advancing greater equality and opportunity.

Strong, hands-on familiarity with IBM MDM Server, IBM InfoSphere Server (IIS) - DataStage/Quality Stage and Information Analyzer is a plus.

Prior experience leading MDM data solutions that manage Party, Product and Location models.

Demonstrable knowledge of Data Warehousing, ETL, Business Intelligence concepts

Hands-on experience with enterprise data warehouse implementations, integrating multiple legacy and strategic source systems.

Understanding of complex SQL queries, inner and joins, and sub-queries.

Analytical assistance and supervision, including quantitative and qualitative data analysis, the evaluation of best practices, and the identification and assessment of operational improvement opportunities at different agencies.

Excellent analytical and communication skills.

Experience in modeling techniques and solutions in dimensional data models.

  Education and Experience

Master's degree in information systems, technology or equivalent preferred with 3-5 years of experience in data integration and analytics.
